About Me

//

My name is Donal O'Sullivan. I create visually striking, information-rich media content for my clients with the express purpose of promoting their businesses.

Competetive Rates

By using newer digital technology, I'm able to provide clients with a level of quality that previously cost far more to produce.

Short-Form Focus

Attention is currency. Beyond making your product or service look great, I aim to present your message in the most captivating and concise way possible.

Flexible and Quick

I utilize high-end, lightweight equipment and a small crew (when necessary) to minimise the amount of production time it takes for on-site video shoots.

Fast Turnaround Times

Expedited projects can be delivered within 48 hours from the day they're shot. Normal projects usually take a week.

What I Do

//

From attention-grabbing Kickstarter videos to eye-popping instagram clips, I can get your message out there in a way that makes it look, sound, and feel compelling.

Short-Form Videos

My specialty is creating videos that last anywhere from fifteen seconds to ten minutes, specifically designed for the web and mobile. I aim to produce content that is clear, catchy, and concise.

Promo Content

Sometimes all you need is something for your website or social media that displays how awesome your business is. I can easily create short, beautiful clips that present you in the best light.

Product Demo

Do you need funding for your new app? Did you design a robot that pets indoor cats without making them upset? I can help present, explain, and animate what you're offering to the world.

Music Video

Video produced for the non-profit coalition GCCA (Global Call for Climate Action)

TckTckTck Spoken Word Video

GCCA member Terisa Siagatonu was one of the winners of the Spoken Word for the World competition to perform her poetry live at the UN climate summit in Paris.

Weeks before the climate summit, GCCA contacted me to produce a video for her winning poem. They needed a fast turnaround time in order to publish the video and submit it to the UN as well as the media. The video was featured on TckTckTck.org, YouTube, CNN.com and NBCNews.com.

  • GCCA's Website: tcktcktck.org
  • CNN link: edition.cnn.com
  • NBC link: NBCNews.com
